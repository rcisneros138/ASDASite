The Social Committee organizes events and activities throughout the year that both raise money for ASDA and provide a great outlet from classes! Many new ideas will be coming to life this year and may include a Flag Football Tournament, Wine and Cheese Tasting, and Group Trivia Nights. Currently the Social Committee organizes holiday parties, sporting event tailgates, the Thriller at Miller Bags Tournament, a Rock Paper Scissors Tournament, and many others! This is the committee for anyone who likes to have an awesome time and help plan big group get-togethers!
