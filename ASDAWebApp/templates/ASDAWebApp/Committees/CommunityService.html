Sara Mogensen (D3)

The Community Outreach Committee is committed to serving the people in need in the greater Milwaukee area. We are currently working with multiple youth programs, teaching health awareness and oral hygiene. The committee collaborates with City on a Hill Health Clinic each month. At this clinic, students teach oral hygiene, answer dental questions and concerns, direct people to dental care facilities and distribute oral hygiene kits at no cost. This year we hope to take the City on a Hill Health Clinic to the next level, dedicating one clinic day a month at CDC-North to provide free dental care to the City on a Hill attendees. We are also planning on increasing the number of after school programs that we work with.

Serving The Community

Our is committed to serving the people in need in the greater Milwaukee area. We collaborate with City on a Hill Health Clinic each month. At this clinic, students teach oral hygiene, answer dental questions and concerns, direct people to dental care facilities and distribute oral hygiene kits at no cost. During the day students refer those interested

to one of Marquette’s Community Care Clinics where they are able to receive free screenings and have the potential to become patients at the school. We also collaborate with youth outreach including tutoring programs, Girls on the Run, Al Brigg’s Run, and oral hygiene instruction to schools and community centers around Milwaukee.
