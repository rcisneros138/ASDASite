As the name of the committee implies, Abraam and Kaitlin are in charge of brainstorming and implementing creative fundraising ideas to raise money for the #marquetteasda chapter! Many ideas are currently in the works for the 2014-2015 school year, including our annual events, like the Amazing Dental Race and Mr. Molar, as well as some exciting new ones! This year, the committee hopes to increase school involvement in ASDA activities, improve on fundraising from last year, and create new events that can be held annually for the MUSoD community!
