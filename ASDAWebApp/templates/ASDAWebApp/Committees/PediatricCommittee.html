This committee is dedicated to those individuals who are interested in pursuing specialties in the dental field, or furthering their education in general dentistry through a GPR or AEGD. The specialty committee is currently under a reconstruction phase and in the process of planning great new things. A goal for this year is to provide exposure, information and contact opportunities within the various specialties, as well as tools for the application process into these programs.
