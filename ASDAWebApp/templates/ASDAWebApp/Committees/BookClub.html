Lets Talk Books

When you read a book, do you ever imagine YOU are any one of the characters within them? Do you ever ponder how YOU would act in the situations the characters get themselves into?

Whether you read it from a paper copy or on your kindle or on your iPad or listen to it on audiotape, come check out our #marquetteasda Book Club. We meet a few times a semester and would love to have YOU join us for our next meeting!

Lets Get Together

Book club gets together once per semester, and we'd love you have you at your next meeting!

To get email updates ont the next meeting, contact Katie or Cecilia!
